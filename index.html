Pig Game Rules
  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
  • In each turn, a player rolls 2 dices at a time as many times as he/ she whishes. Each result get added to his/her ROUND score
  • BUT the player looses his current score when one of 2 dices is a 1. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • A player looses his ENTIRE score when he/she rolls two 6 in a row. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  • The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his/ her ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • The first player to reach the score they put in first will win the game !
